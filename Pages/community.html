Get Involved In Your Community

Communities as a whole are impacted by disasters. Participating actively in disaster preparedness and response initiatives is one way to make the Philippines stronger and safer. Whether it's raising awareness, volunteering, or lending a hand to neighbors in need, everyone has a part to play. During emergencies, little acts can save lives and lessen damage when they are spread throughout a community.

Ways to Get Involved

1

Learn about local dangers, warning signals, and safety protocols.

2

Make emergency supplies for your home and urge friends and family to follow suit.

3

Participate in community exercises or local disaster risk reduction initiatives as a volunteer.

4

During disasters, provide assistance to vulnerable populations, such as the elderly, children, or those with disabilities.

5

During emergencies, disseminate verifiable information from reliable sources to avoid false information.

6

Encourage neighborhood projects that lower the danger of disasters, such as tree planting, flood control, or cleanup campaigns.

Beyond quick fixes, it's critical to cultivate a culture of preparedness and community cooperation. Encourage neighbors to work together to create backup plans, take part in neighborhood exercises, and communicate during emergencies. You can contribute to the resilience and safety of your entire community in addition to protecting yourself by remaining informed, prepared, and connected.
