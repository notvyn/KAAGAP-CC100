WELCOME TO KAAGAP!

Your reliable partner for Disaster Preparedness, Safety, and Knowledge.

Disasters can strike anytime, leaving many communities vulnerable. KAAGAP helps Filipinos stay ready with clear guidance, practical safety steps, and accessible resources. By promoting awareness and responsible habits, we aim to build a resilient, empowered, and safer nation.

About Us

We provide clear, reliable information to help communities stay safe and make smart decisions during emergencies. In a country prone to typhoons, earthquakes, and floods, being prepared is essential. This site offers practical tips and resources to help every Filipino stay ready and resilient. Together, we can build a safer community.
